Our Key Programs

1. Drug Abuse Awareness Campaign

Educating young people on the dangers of drug abuse and helping them find safe paths away from addiction.

2. Skills Acquisition Workshops

We provide hands-on training in tailoring, ICT, carpentry, and more to equip youths with job-ready skills.

3. Moral & Civic Education

Through mentorship and community events, we instill discipline, cultural values, and leadership mindset.
