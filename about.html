Who We Are

Bichi Youth Development Alliance (BYDA) is a youth-driven organization founded by passionate young people in Bichi Local Government Area. Our mission is to address challenges affecting our community, especially those facing the youth.

Our Mission

To empower, educate, and engage the youth of Bichi towards positive transformation through awareness, skill acquisition, and mentorship.

Our Vision

A self-reliant, morally upright, and educated youth population leading the future of Bichi with integrity and innovation.
